Welcome to DBPHCC!

  dbPHCC is a database(db) for the prognosis(P) of hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC). It is a system for all the experimentally identified molecules which are related to HCC prognosis. It was curated from 1479 records in 645 literature articles which were published in the past 13 (2002-2014) years.
  Eventually the dbPHCC collected 320 proteins, 154 genes, 88 miRNAs, 6 Methylations, 8 Phosphorylations and 5 others, some items were recorded multiple times.

Feature:

  • Focusing on hepatocellular carcinoma prognosis molecules;
  • All date came from experimental papers, rendering our date more credible;
  • Molecules of different levels can verify each other;
  • Comprehensive and reliable analyses from multiple perspectives.
